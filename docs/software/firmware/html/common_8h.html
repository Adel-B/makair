◆ CycleSubPhases

enum CycleSubPhases

Defines the 4 phases of the respiratory cycle.

Enumerator
INSPIRATION 

Push inhalation:

  • Send air until the peak pressure is reached
  • The blower valve is open so the air stream goes to the patient's lungs
HOLD_INSPIRATION 

Hold inhalation:

  • Once the peak pressure is reached, the pressure is hold to the plateau pressure The pressure has been decreasing for more than 0.1 s.
  • The 2 valves are hold closed
EXHALE 

Exhalation:

  • The patient exhales
HOLD_EXHALE 

Hold exhalation:

  • Valves stay closed as the pressure is below the PEEP
